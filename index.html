Basics of
Networking

Networking connects devices to share data and resources, enabling seamless communication within homes and across the globe. Key components include routers, switches, and modems, which manage and direct data traffic. IP addresses uniquely identify each device, while protocols like TCP/IP ensure reliable data transfer. Understanding these basics helps you appreciate how your digital interactions are facilitated and protected.
